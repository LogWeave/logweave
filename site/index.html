See exactly what changed after a deploy

Anchor any query to a deployment marker and instantly see new patterns, spikes, and resolved issues. No manual log trawling — LogWeave shows you the signal.

New patterns are tagged NEW. Existing patterns with sudden volume increases are tagged SPIKE. Patterns that went quiet are marked resolved.

Cut your logging bill without guessing

LogWeave automatically identifies high-volume patterns that are driving up your Datadog, CloudWatch, or Loki costs. Every pattern is scored by volume percentage — the noisy ones surface to the top.

"Email sent: 46.5% of notifications-svc volume" — one pattern, nearly half your bill. Your AI can query this directly and recommend exactly where to add sampling.
